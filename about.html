Our Story

Connecting Travelers with Authentic Experiences

At LuxAndra Travel, we are more than just a travel agency; we are storytellers and experience curators. Featured Destination: Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Known as the ‘Yoga Capital of the World’, Rishikesh is nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas along the holy Ganges river. It’s a place where spiritual tranquility and adventure sports coexist. Whether you're an adventure seeker looking for rafting and trekking or a spiritual traveler seeking yoga and meditation retreats, Rishikesh offers an unforgettable experience.
